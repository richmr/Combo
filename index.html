About the Uncombinator Local

Uncombinator thinks humans are lazy when they scramble combination locks. Take a reading of the combination visible on a lock after it is scrambled and enter it in the data fields. Do this enough times and you can greatly reduce the entropy in the combination.

See my talk at the TOOOL Lock Picking Village at DC 26: [TBD]
